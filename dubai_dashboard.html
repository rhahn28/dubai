🚐 Caravan / Convoy Evacuation — Saudi Route

CARAVAN PLANNING: If departing by road convoy, coordinate with other families for safety.

  • Vehicle prep: Full tank + 20L jerry can (desert stretch: 450km between stations). Minimum 10L water per person.
  • Convoy size: 3–5 vehicles ideal. Single car risky in desert breakdown. Share mechanic tools, tow rope.
  • Route: Dubai → Abu Dhabi (E11, 1.5hr) → Al Ghuwaifat border (2hr) → Al Hofuf (3hr) → Riyadh (3hr)
  • Rest stops: Abu Dhabi (last reliable stop for fuel/food), Al Hofuf (Saudi side, first major city ~3hr from border)
  • Night driving: Avoid. Desert highway has camel crossings, limited lighting, no cell coverage in stretches.
  • Communications: Download offline Google Maps for Saudi Arabia. WhatsApp groups with convoy. Have a satellite messenger if possible.
  • Documents: Passport, Saudi visa (or apply for e-visa at visa.visitsaudi.com), vehicle registration, insurance, UAE exit stamp.
  • Timing: Depart pre-dawn (4–5 AM) to reach border by 7 AM and Riyadh by evening.

⚠️ SAUDI ROUTE RISKS:

  • 🟥 Eastern Province targets: Saudi Aramco HQ (Dhahran), Ghawar oil field (world’s largest), Ras Tanura refinery, Abqaiq processing — all high-value military targets. Houthi/Iran have struck these before (2019 Abqaiq–Khurais attack).
  • 🟥 Dammam/Dhahran metro area: 1.5M people, major military base. If conflict escalates to Saudi targets, Eastern Province is frontline.
  • 🟧 Desert crossing: 450+ km of open desert between Al Ghuwaifat and Al Hofuf. Limited services, extreme heat, no cell coverage.
  • 🟧 Visa uncertainty: Saudi may restrict entry during crisis or require special transit permits. Check before departing.
  • 🟧 Border congestion: During a regional crisis, expect massive queues at Al Ghuwaifat. Plan 2–4hr wait at border minimum.
  • 🟩 Riyadh is safer: 400km inland from Persian Gulf coast, away from oil infrastructure, has diplomatic quarter with embassies.
  • 🟩 RUH airport: Major international hub with global connections. RUH → London, Frankfurt, Cairo, Istanbul, Karachi all available.

VERDICT: Saudi route is BACKUP ONLY. Use Oman (Hatta/Muscat) as primary. Saudi route only if: (1) Oman border closed, (2) you have Saudi visa, (3) you can join a convoy of 3+ vehicles. Riyadh is the destination — avoid stopping in Eastern Province.
